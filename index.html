About Us

As third generation restaurant owners, we are proud of our heritage as well as the values set by our hardworking grandparents.

We are passionated about the food we make and the process of creating. As a coastal family, we have always been near the sea and all the great things it provides.

The sea is our greatest source of inspiration and the place where we spend most of our leasure time.

Services

We serve three different lunch alternatives every day. All of which have an ingridient of the sea. Our lunch meals can be served as both gluten and/or lactose free.

Our services include catering any small to mid sized event. For catering menu's, we provide additional meal alternatives which can be customized depending on the type of event and any preferences.

See our contact information at the bottom of the page and let us know how we can create an amazing menu for your important day.
